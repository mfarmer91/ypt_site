May 1, 2017 Man, when homemade pasta -- that doesn't suck -- hits your lips for the first time, you can't just stop at one serving. I am saddened that I did not think to develop pasta making skills sooner, as freshly-made egg noodles enhance one's quality of life. This second, successful attempt at making pasta functions as my second exam in pasta-making 101. I made at A minus. Hey, at this point, I'll take it. The pasta was actually edible, and I think other fools will respect my technique.

April 1, 2017 Ye, so, the first time I made pasta, my main mistake was forgetting to re-flour my dough after it was rolled out. That was a foolish blunder. In fact, the lack of white, non-cocaine, cooking dust was so great, that the end result of my initial pasta-making journey was pure failure. For comparison, mind map the final scene in Lord of the Rings: Return of the King. My technique's embodiment was Smeagle; foolishly following the ring, my pasta, into the boiling water, and heating into nothingness. The painstakingly rolled and folded raw dough was compressed into disks as I cut the noodles -- still hopeful.
